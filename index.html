About Pratik
“I don’t just design —
I build worlds
worth inhabiting.”

Born and raised in Dhaka, I grew up obsessed with how things look, feel, and communicate. That obsession became a discipline spanning graphic design, photography, brand strategy, event direction, and entrepreneurship.

I believe great design is leadership with a visual language. I’ve built organizations, led creative teams, directed large-scale events, and crafted brand identities that endure. My work lives at the intersection of art and impact.

Currently based in Uttara, Dhaka — building toward international creative presence. The work speaks first.
